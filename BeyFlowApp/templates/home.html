HOW TRANSFERS ARE DONE

Users can deposit funds into their online accounts via a bey flow mobile cash teller or ATM.

Automatically, funds in the account will be converted from fiat currency into it's digital secured fiat form.

Funds are immediately available for transfer. They choose another user to send their funds to and confirm transaction of sent amount.

Said funds are now in beneficiary's account. Beneficiary of funds can find a mobile cash teller to withdraw from their account.
